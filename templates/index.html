Tutorial: Exporting WhatsApp Chat

Step 1: Open WhatsApp

Open the WhatsApp application on your mobile device.

Step 2: Open the Chat

Go to the chat that you want to export.

Step 3: Tap on Options

Tap on the options menu in the chat window (usually three dots or a gear icon).

Step 4: Select More

Select "More" or any similar option from the menu.

Step 5: Export Chat

Choose the "Export Chat" option from the menu.

Step 6: Choose Export Method

Choose whether to export with or without media and select your preferred method (email, etc.).
